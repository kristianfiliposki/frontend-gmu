This section includes the global currencies as well as:

  • FXbtc = The price of Bitcoin in USD
  • FXgold = The price of gold in USD per standard unit (i.d. ounce)
  • Pbtc = The number of people who actively use BTC as a medium of exchange, store of value, or for payments
  • Pgold = The population that holds physical or digital gold (e.g. 15–20 million worldwide)
  • Sbtc, Sgold = The estimated currency stability (i.d. 0.70 for BTC, 0.98 for gold)
