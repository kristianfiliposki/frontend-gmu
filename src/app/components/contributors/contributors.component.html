Why Support Us?

The Global Money Index is an independent and transparent initiative to create a real-time monetary benchmark, combining fiat and crypto data from reliable sources. To grow, we need people who believe in the vision of a more neutral and decentralized economic system.

What is the GMU Token?

The GMU Token is a support token. It is not tradable and cannot be resold. Purchasing the token means funding the project and becoming a part of our community.

Note: The GMU Token is not a financial instrument or investment product. It is a digital proof of support.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I resell the GMU Token?

No. The token is non-transferable and non-tradable. It is purely a way to support the project and unlock benefits.

Is this a financial investment?

No. The GMU Token does not promise financial returns. It is a donation/support token with community rewards.

What wallet do I need to receive it?

Any EVM-compatible wallet (e.g., MetaMask) will work. After purchasing, the token is airdropped to your address.
